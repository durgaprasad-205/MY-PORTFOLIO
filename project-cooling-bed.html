Project Overview

This innovative cooling bed system was designed to address heat accumulation issues in healthcare settings, particularly for patients with limited mobility. The system uses advanced heat dissipation techniques to maintain optimal surface temperature while being energy efficient.

Development Process

The project began with extensive research into thermal management solutions for healthcare environments. After prototyping several designs, we settled on a modular approach that allowed for easy customization based on patient needs. The final product underwent rigorous testing in simulated hospital conditions.

Results & Impact

The cooling bed system demonstrated significant improvements in patient comfort and reduced the incidence of heat-related complications. Hospital staff reported easier patient management and reduced need for manual repositioning to prevent overheating.
